Frequently Asked Questions

What types of questions can Cheatly answer?

Cheatly uses advanced AI vision to analyze screenshots, so it can handle virtually any question type: multiple choice, multiple select, fill-in-the-blank, matching, short answer, essay questions, math problems, and more.

How fast is Cheatly?

Answers typically appear in 2-5 seconds, depending on the complexity of the question and your internet speed. The AI processes a screenshot of your screen and returns the answer almost instantly.

What platforms does it work on?

Cheatly works on any website that runs in Chrome — Canvas, Blackboard, Google Forms, Quizlet, Coursera, edX, and any other web-based platform.

Do I need my own API key?

No! Cheatly works out of the box. We handle all the AI processing on our end so you can just install and start using it immediately — no setup required.

Is my data secure?

Cheatly sends screenshots to our secure API for AI processing. No data is stored permanently. Screenshots are analyzed in real-time and immediately discarded.

How do the display modes work?

Invisible: The answer is copied to your clipboard silently — nothing appears on screen. Sneaky: A tiny dot appears in the corner that expands on hover. Homework: A full overlay panel shows the answer with a loading animation.
